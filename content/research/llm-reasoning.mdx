# LLMs as Reasoning Engines

Current state-of-the-art models demonstrate significant jumps in multi-hop reasoning.

## Abstract

We explore the transition from statistical pattern matching to structured logic within large-scale transformer architectures.

## Methodology

Evaluated across GSM8K and MATH datasets using varying prompt templates.

## Results

Significant correlation between parameter count and reasoning depth.
