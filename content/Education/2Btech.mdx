# Final Year Thesis

## FBG based Inclination Sensor with real-time monitoring 
- The experimental setup used an FBG, NodeMCU, Firebase, and an Android App for monitoring.
- The FBG reflection spectrum was characterized, showing a minima at 1545.29nm and maxima at 1544.95nm.
- Challenges like mechanical inaccuracy and voltage offset were solved with curve fitting and a voltage divider circuit.
- Reflected power was linear from 1.30 degrees, mapping angle to photodetector output voltage.
- The system achieved real-time monitoring, updating angle, strain, and voltage to Firebase every 10 seconds.
